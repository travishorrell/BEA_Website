About Us

Welcome to the Bonneville Education Association (BEA), a vital part of the Idaho Education Association and a proud member of the National Education Association. Our mission at BEA is to significantly enhance the lives of educators, staff, and all individuals dedicated to the educational journey within the Bonneville School District. We strive to advance education for all, ensuring every student benefits from an exceptional learning experience. Dedicated to fostering professional excellence among educators, BEA champions improved salaries, benefits, and working conditions through diligent negotiations. As the exclusive representative of School District 93, we engage in continuous dialogue with the School Board, advocating for our members’ voices to be heard, respected, and acted upon. Our unity empowers us to address matters critical to the teaching profession with a cohesive voice, ensuring both individual and collective interests are presented with clarity and strength before the Board of Trustees. Affiliated with both the Idaho Education Association and the National Education Association, we stand as a beacon of support, advocacy, and professional growth for those who are shaping the future of education in our community.
